    What is 3D Printing?

    3D printing is a manufacturing process when material is layered on top of each other to form a custom shape. These materials include polymers, ceramic, and metals. This process allows for high quality designs to be manufactured in a timely, automated manner. 3D printing can make anything the user desires. It could be a mechanical part or a novelty item. 3D printing can also make parts that may be impossible to make with other manufacturing methods; the possibilities are limitless, making this process an efficient method of manufacturing!
